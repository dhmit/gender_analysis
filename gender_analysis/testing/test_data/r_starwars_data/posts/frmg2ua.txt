He finds his father in the depths of Coruscant. A grizzled cop with a limp, a cheaply made robotic arm, and a taste for vengeance. They work together to track down the scum that killed his mother and stole Finn as a baby, selling him off to the First Order as payback for his father's strangely effective police work until the sweaty nightmares of betrayal, flashes of lightning on an old man's gleefully screaming face, and falling forever, spark a ghostly memory of a long forgotten time.....
